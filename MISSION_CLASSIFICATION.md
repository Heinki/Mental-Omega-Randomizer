# Mission Build Classification

This is the reviewed classification used by seed generation. `true` means the
mission is played through fixed/scripted units, heroes, or map-provided powers
without a player-operated production base. It does not mean the mission is
easy. `false` means the player owns, receives, captures, or operates production
at some point.

Classification uses reviewed gameplay behavior and community correction rather
than treating every mission without initial player production as baseless. For
example, Allied 01 is explicitly `false`.
